✦ About Me

Art is how I
translate feelings

Hi, I'm Aayushi — an artist, student, and creator based in India. I've loved art since I can remember, and over the years I've found my language in paintings, pencil sketches, and handcrafted DIY pieces.

I run Brush it Off — my art channel where I share my journey, process videos, and finished pieces. Right now I'm also exploring how AI can be a creative partner — from AI-generated art to blending technology with traditional craft.
