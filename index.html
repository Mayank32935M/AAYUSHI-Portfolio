The Artist

A painter who speaks in color and culture.

Aayushi is a self-taught painter who has developed a uniquely vibrant and culturally charged artistic identity. Her work explores the intersection of Indian folk traditions and bold contemporary aesthetics — from devotional imagery and mandala patterns to abstract cubist figures.

My Process

How each painting
comes to life.

01

Concept & Research

Every artwork starts with a story. Whether drawn from mythology, daily life, or abstract emotions — the idea is deeply researched and felt before any brush touches the canvas.

02

Sketching & Composition

The layout is hand-sketched to establish bold visual compositions, dynamic proportions, and intentional negative space that gives the painting room to breathe.

03

Color & Layering

Colors are never chosen carelessly. Aayushi layers pigment with deliberate emotional intent — building warmth, contrast, and harmony that speaks before words can.

04

Details & Finishing

The final stage is an intimate conversation between the artist and the piece — refining contours, adding texture, and sealing it with the quiet confidence of a signature.
